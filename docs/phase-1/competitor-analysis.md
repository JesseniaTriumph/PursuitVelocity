# Competitor Analysis & Market Landscape

## Executive Summary

Pursuit Sync occupies a narrow but powerful niche: a community OS for underrepresented tech talent leaving intensive fellowship programs. No direct competitor serves this exact population with this feature depth.

## Pursuit-Specific Advantages

1. **Captive audience**: Pursuit has ~200 active fellows per cohort and thousands of alumni. Velocity has first-mover advantage within this network.
2. **Trust signal**: Fellows have completed a rigorous 12-month program. Quality floor is high.
3. **Staff + mentor bridge**: Events, workshops, and office hours can be staff-seeded, giving the feed immediate value.
4. **No cold start**: Pursuit can mandate onboarding as part of the fellowship curriculum.

## 5 Whys: Why Fellows Don't Collaborate

1. **Why don't fellows collaborate more?** → They don't know who else is building what.
2. **Why don't they know?** → There's no structured visibility layer after cohort ends.
3. **Why is there no visibility?** → Pursuit's current tools (Slack, Google Sheets) aren't built for discovery.
4. **Why haven't those tools been replaced?** → No budget/product team dedicated to internal tooling.
5. **Why no dedicated product team?** → The fellows themselves haven't shipped something internal until now.

**Root cause: No community infrastructure purpose-built for Pursuit's network.**
